The meeting will be held on January 15 in P405. We will be discussing the history of BBA, what it is about, answer questions, and hold an election.

Executive Board Election

You must be an IBIT, BIT or BTM student to run for a role and vote. You are allowed to run for more than one role, but you can only settle for one. Students in term 1 cannot run for president. The new executive team will have access to previous members of the executive board for support, advice, and mentorship. Joining the team will help you further your communication, planning and management skills. If you wish to run for a position, please have a short introduction about yourself ready on the day of the elections. We will be asking you to introduce yourself before the election begins.

Available roles:
  • President

    Be the head of the BBA, interact with staff and industry members. Plan meetings for executives and work closely with the Vice Pres. to achieve goals set by the whole group. Check up on members doing projects to see if they need support, and help them find it.

  • Vice - President

    Work complimentary with presidential roles and responsibilities while at times acting as interim President. Additionally engage with the Executive team/committee as needed.

  • Secretary

    Oversee the BBA email and all incorporated communicative activities. Record minutes for the BBA executive and general meetings.

  • Treasurer

    Manage all financial responsibilities and activities by planning, reporting, and budgeting.

  • Members at Large

    Participate in executive meetings and assist with BBA activities and projects.

The BBA is a team. As a team we have capabilities to accomplish things we could never do as individuals; be a part of something bigger, be apart of our team.
