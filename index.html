Bits and Bytes Association

Fall 2020

The Bits and Bytes Association (BBA) was created to improve communication between students and staff to enhance the quality of education for Introduction to Business Information Technology (IBIT), Business Information Technology (BIT), Business Technology Management (BTM) and Information Security (IS) programs offered at Red River College through the Applied Computer Education (ACE) department. Since its inception in 2015, BBA has grown to over 100 members today and continutes to climb steadily. If you would like to join the Bits and Bytes, You can join our Discord!
